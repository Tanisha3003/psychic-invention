            <h2>Welcome to the Future of Vikshit Bharat</h2>
            <p>As India strives to achieve its ambitious vision of becoming a Vikshit Bharat (developed India) by 2047, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is emerging as a powerful tool for accelerating and transforming its development journey. This introductory section sets the stage for exploring the multifaceted ways AI can contribute to this national aspiration.</p>
            <img src="Pictures/bg1.jpg" width="25%" height="25%" class="centered-image">

                <strong>What is Vikshit Bharat?</strong>
                
                <p>Vikshit Bharat translates to "developed India" and embodies the nation's vision to achieve:</p>
                
                <p><em>Economic prosperity:</em> A robust, knowledge-based economy with sustainable growth and improved living standards for all citizens.</p>
                <p><em>Social inclusion:</em> An equitable society where every individual has equal opportunities to access education, healthcare, and other essential services.</p>
                <p><em>Technological advancement:</em> Leveraging cutting-edge technologies like AI to drive innovation and solve critical development challenges.</p>

                <strong>Why is AI crucial for Vikshit Bharat?</strong>
                
                <p>The rapid advancements in AI present a unique opportunity for India to:</p>
                
                <p><em>Revolutionize existing industries:</em> AI-powered solutions can improve efficiency, optimize resource utilization, and enhance productivity across various sectors.</p>
                <p><em>Address complex challenges:</em> AI can analyze vast amounts of data to identify patterns, predict future trends, and develop innovative solutions to critical issues like climate change, poverty, and healthcare access.</p>
                <p><em>Empower citizens:</em> AI has the potential to personalize services, bridge the digital divide, and create a more inclusive and equitable society.</p>
